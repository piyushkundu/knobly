The History of the Internet

Exploring the Journey of Connectivity

Overview

The Internet is a vast network of networks that has changed the world significantly since its inception. Below are the major phases in its evolution:

1960s
The initial concept for a network was developed, leading to ARPANET.
1980s
TCP/IP protocol was adopted, laying the groundwork for global networking.
1990s
The World Wide Web was introduced, making information accessible.

Internet Components

  • Web Browsers
  • Email Services
  • Social Networks
